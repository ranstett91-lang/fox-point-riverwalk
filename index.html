The Opportunity

A rare public river edge inside a hard-working industrial coast.

From Claymont through Fox Point and north toward the steel and port corridor, the Delaware River shoreline is low, altered, and shaped by rail, roads, fill, and heavy industry. Public access is rare. That is what makes Fox Point so important.

Fox Point can become the place where a new waterfront story begins: safe access, stronger shoreline design, public learning, and long-term community investment in one visible project.

Investment Thesis

One catalytic investment can create a public riverfront, strengthen the shoreline, and unlock the next round of funding.

Multi-Pronged Investment

What a foundation investment can buy

This is not a one-lane project. The same investment can create public benefit, stronger environmental performance, and a much more fundable capital story.

1. Public experience

Trail access, river overlooks, safer circulation, and a memorable civic destination.

2. Environmental value
