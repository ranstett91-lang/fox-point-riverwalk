Why This Matters To Amtrak

A real corridor problem needs a visible response.

Amtrak’s own climate work says this corridor is getting hotter, wetter, and more exposed. Wilmington is a sea-level-rise hot spot. Fox Point gives Amtrak a nearby place to make resilience visible and useful.

Multi-Pronged Investment

What an Amtrak-aligned investment can buy

This is not only a riverwalk. The same investment can strengthen resilience, improve public experience, and create a stronger long-term funding story around the corridor.

1. Corridor resilience

Stronger shoreline thinking, water management, and site design that respond to a stressed edge.

2. Public-facing benefit

Trail access, overlooks, safer circulation, and a place people can connect to the river.

3. Stronger narrative

A public story that translates technical reports into trust, credibility, and momentum.

4. Funding leverage

A stronger concept package for grants, settlements, agency support, and future capital partners.
