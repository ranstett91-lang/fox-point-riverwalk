Capital Already Identified

$4.3M is already in the pipeline.

Three separate legal agreements, NRD settlements, and mitigation requirements already point to Fox Point. None require a cold start.

$2.5M

Heffernan Bond Bill

Contingent

Wetland creation + park improvements

Rep. Heffernan pledged $3M in bond funds to satisfy state mitigation requirements for the new Edgemoor port. $2.5M designated for Fox Point wetland and park improvements. Triggered when port construction begins and Diamond State Port Corp. approves.

$1M

APU / DNREC

Active

Living shoreline restoration — Fox Point Cove

American Premier Underwriters settlement with DNREC. Agreements in place. A Natural Resource Damage Cooperative Assessment determined the value; the outcome is a planned living shoreline ecological restoration in Fox Point Cove (DARP).

$808K

Chemours / NOAA

Future Process

Environmental restoration — Titanium Dioxide legacy

Joint federal/state NRD settlement from Chemours' TiO₂ facility at Edgemoor. NOAA is lead trustee. $808K for a joint restoration project. No site selected yet — will require public participation. Fox Point is a natural candidate.
